Hi, I'm
Andre Fortou.

I'm an Industrial & Interaction Designer graduating from Syracuse University's College of Visual & Performing Arts in 2026. My work spans medical wearables, smart product design, sustainable materials, and biomimetic structures.

I care about design that starts with a real human problem. Whether it's helping an ostomate sleep through the night or rethinking what a coastal seawall could be, I try to find solutions that are honest about their materials and meaningful in their impact.
